A few words about me

Hi, I'm Tim. I've always had a passion for computers and technology. Over the years, I created several game plugins that added new features and enhancements. Then one day, I came up with an exciting game concept: World Evolution.

I planned everything out and began developing it, but quickly realized it was more challenging than I had expected. I decided to put the project on hold, but the idea never left my mind.

Later, when I realized that I wanted to pursue a career as a game and software developer, I picked up the project again. I've been working on it ever since, and one day, I hope to bring it to life.
